BONE MEAL

Bone meal is a valuable organic fertilizer widely utilized in bio farming for its rich nutrient composition and soil conditioning attributes. Derived from ground animal bones, particularly cattle bones, bone meal is prized for its high phosphorus and calcium content, both vital for robust plant growth. In bio farming, bone meal serves as a reliable source of phosphorus, facilitating healthy root development, robust flowering, and fruiting in crops. Its slow-release nature ensures a consistent supply of phosphorus to plants, minimizing nutrient leaching and runoff concerns. Additionally, the calcium content in bone meal contributes to soil pH balance and enhances plant cell structure, promoting disease resistance and overall plant vigor. Moreover, bone meal aids in improving soil structure and microbial activity, fostering optimal conditions for beneficial soil organisms to thrive. By incorporating bone meal into their farming practices, bio farmers can effectively enhance soil fertility, promote sustainable agriculture, and improve crop yield and quality.
